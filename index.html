Aviation Accident Insights

Introduction

Welcome to Aviation Accident Insights, a comprehensive analysis of aviation accidents and their relationship with airport traffic data. This project aims to uncover patterns and trends that can inform safety improvements in the aviation industry.

We integrated two datasets: one detailing aviation accidents (SDV_Project_Dataset.csv) and another on airport traffic (Aiports2.csv). Through data cleaning, transformation, and visualization, we explore key questions about accident severity, locations, aircraft types, and traffic impacts. This webpage presents our findings through interactive visualizations, combining Power BI dashboards and Plotly charts to provide a dynamic storytelling experience.

Power BI Analysis (Questions 1-5)

The following questions were explored using a Power BI dashboard, providing an overview of accident trends and factors:

  • Q1: How do accidents vary over time?
  • Q2: Which weather conditions lead to more severe accidents?
  • Q3: What are the top locations for accidents?
  • Q4: Which aircraft maker has the most accidents?
  • Q5: How does engine count influence accident severity?
